title: 'PACT API'
---

Variable provides a PACT conformant API for sharing your Product Carbon Footprint (PCF) data with your customers.

## PACT: Partnership for Carbon Transparency
PACT is an open standard for sharing Product Carbon Footprint data. You can read more about it here: https://www.carbon-transparency.org

### Rate limit
To ensure the best performance for all users, we have a rate limit of __10 requests per second__.
